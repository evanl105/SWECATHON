Summary

We’ll build a platform where students share real interview experiences. When you add your story, you unlock a growing library of up-to-date details—how many rounds there are, what each round covers, sample questions, and tips from past candidates.

Problem

  • Many students rely on old question banks or forum posts that may be out of date.

  • Some sites hide details behind paywalls or require special access.
  • Students need a simple, open resource that stays fresh. By sharing their own interviews, everyone gets access to the latest, most accurate insights.
